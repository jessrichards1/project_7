Artrepreneurs Boston

Artrepreneurs Boston is an independently funded non-profit consulting agency dedicated to creating and facilitating partnerships between Boston based for-profit + non-profit sectors in order to add value to the local creative economy. In addition to the collaboration, we provide services such as marketing and public relations expertise for for-profits and non-profits to expand their potential and make them thrive.

We work with Boston based start-up companies to plan a Corporate Social Responsibility platform and work towards eventual B Corporation certification.

Goals:
+ Attract, foster and retain entrepreneurial talent and small business in the city of Boston
+ Community collaboration and partnerships
+ Embed the for-profit sector into the cultural fabric of the Boston community
+ Make the for-profit and non-profit sectors allies along with the private sector
+ Expand non-profitsâ€™ capacity to serve its community
+ Help small for-profit businesses establish themselves in the community that they serve while advising B Corp certification down the road
We believe in the need and value of balancing profits with purpose. Out of the hundreds of Certified B Corporations globally, only 13 of these companies are located in the city of Boston. There are immense benefits of having a local economy mainly fueled by B Corps.
